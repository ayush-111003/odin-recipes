lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and often meat or vegetables. To prepare it, start by making a rich meat sauce. Heat olive oil in a large pan, sauté finely chopped onions and garlic until soft, then add ground beef or Italian sausage, cooking until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried herbs like oregano and basil, and season with salt and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes to develop flavor. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside. Next, assemble the lasagna. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of ricotta cheese mixed with beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, and chopped parsley. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese, then repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with a top layer of meat sauce and mozzarella. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-40 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving to allow the layers to set. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!

ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta sheets and drain.
  3. Brown ground beef or sausage with garlic and onion.
  4. Mix ricotta, eggs, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, layer sauce, pasta, cheese mixture, and meat.
  6. Repeat layers, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil and bake 25 more minutes.
  8. Let it rest before serving.
